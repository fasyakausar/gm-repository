Module Summary

This module introduces a separate button to finalize and confirm the sale order only when the product is ready to dispatch. It ensures that delivery orders are created in a proper date-wise sequence, enhancing logistics management and operational clarity. It also customizes the Proforma Invoice to show down payment and remaining amount details.

Key Features

  • Displays down payment and remaining amount in Proforma Invoice footer.

Proforma Footer Enhancement

When printing a Sale Proforma Invoice, this module extends the default template to display financial insights directly in the footer section:

  • Down Payment – Shows the pre-paid amount as entered in the "Other Info" tab.
  • Remaining Amount – Displays the calculated amount due after subtracting the down payment from the total order value.

These values appear only when printing Proforma Invoices, ensuring clean and relevant financial details are available for customer communication.
